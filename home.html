Suffah Welfare Educational Society

​(Logon mein behtar who hai jo doosre logon ko faida pahonchaye)

Assalamualiakum wa rahmatullahi wa barakatahu In Allah The Name Of ALLAH, The Most beneficent and the Most Merciful. SUFFAH WELFARE EDUCATION SOCIETY is the part of Ahle Sunnah Wa'l Jama'at. From time to time, according to the current events or needs of the community at large, this organization publishes educational content to strengthen the faith and beliefs of Sunni Muslims. In easily understandable manner, so that everyone gain maximum benefit and make the hereafter comfortable for themselves. The head of the society, AMEER-E-SUFFAH HAZRAT ALLAMA MAULANA MUFTI MOHAMMAD IMRAN QUADRI SAHAB is working in Hyderabad, Telangana and in many states of India to improve the faith, actions and characters of people, needs no explanation, he deserves our utmost praise and gratitude. We pray that Allah Ta'ala grants this society’s mission acceptance and gives a long life to the SUFFAH Welfare Education Society and accepts the work of ahle-sunnat-wal-jammat and makes SUFFAH's message widespread prominent all over the globe. Aameen Summa Aameen.
